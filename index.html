Your Agent

Built From Real Experience.

Diego's story didn't start in a real estate classroom — it started at 23, when he bought his first rental property. By his third investment, he decided to get his license to level up his own portfolio. Then something shifted: friends and family started asking for his help navigating transactions, and he realized he had a real gift for guiding people through one of the biggest decisions of their lives.

He was working at a bank — stable, safe — but walked away to bet on himself. Since October 2018, he's been doing exactly that as the founder of Cortes & Co. Realty Group, serving buyers, sellers, and investors across the DFW Metroplex.

Diego specializes in first-time buyers, new construction, land, and lake & ranch properties. He's bilingual, deeply connected to the Latino community, and takes pride in making the process feel clear, accessible, and personal — no matter who you are or what you do for a living.
